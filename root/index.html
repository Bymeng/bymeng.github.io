5G and 6G explained

Before the 5G and 6g there was another technologies like 2G or 3.5G, with those the humanity was able to engage human-to-human communication through voice and text Today thanks to the enhasment of these technologies we can have now benefits that in other time we can't, some of these benefits are:

  • Both generations have very low latency
  • 5G and 6G Use two different parts of the spectrum
  • 6G will speed up the internet of things
  • 6G will not replace 5G
Some of the technologies that benefits from 6G are:
  • Internet of things(IoT)
  • Edge computing
  • High-performance computing(HPC)
